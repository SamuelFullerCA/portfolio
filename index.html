About Me

Born and raised on the northern island of New Zealand, I moved to Southern California at the age of 10, I grew up facinated by both the utility and hardware of computers, as well as the digital world. I was always facinated by how I could enter a whole new digital world and virtually be able to connect with my family thousands of miles across the Pacific.

Out of highschool I followed in my fathers footsteps and entered the Audio-Visual world. I worked as an A/V technician for 3 years before realizing I always found myself more interested in the digital engineerig and processing of audio moire than I enjoyed the physical labor of installing it as a technician. This led me to reinvest back into my passion of the digital word.

So here I am as an (soon to be) skilled full-stack web developer finiding passion in developing, fixing, and exploring web applications. My journey to reach this point, where I'm happy and passionate with what I do, has been a long one and I'm looking forward to adding my mark to the every growing and evolving digital universe.
